eSIM Download and Activation Instructions

To check the interactive instructions or follow the steps below click here:

Before you start ensure your phone is connected to the internet with a stable WiFi connection.

Step 1:
iPhones and iPads: On iOS 17 or later, you can install your eSIM directly from the Monty eSIM app in a single click.
On all other iOS devices, open your camera app as if you are going to take a picture and point it at the QR code above.
Follow the prompts to complete the installation.
If you are unable to scan the QR code, go to Settings -> Cellular -> Add Cellular Plan and enter the eSIM activation information manually.


Android and other OS: Go to the Cellular/Mobile network settings of your eSIM-capable device.
Follow the instructions to Add Cellular Plan / Add Mobile Network.

Step 2:
Turn Data Roaming ON.


Step 3:
Disconnect WiFi and wait a few minutes to allow the eSIM to set itself up correctly.

Additional Information:

- While the principles of eSIM activation remain the same, installation flows may vary slightly from one phone to another.
- If your eSIM is not connecting to a network, ensure that Cellular/Mobile Data is assigned to your eSIM and Data Roaming is turned ON.
- If you are connected to a network but the data connection is not working as expected, check if the APN is mentioned in your eSIM profile information. If yes, set the correct APN for your eSIM. Toggle Airplane mode ON and OFF and check the connection again.
